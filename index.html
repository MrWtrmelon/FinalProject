Disc Golf


What is Disc Golf?

Disc golf is a dynamic and accessible sport that combines elements of traditional golf with the precision and skill of frisbee throwing. Players navigate a course, aiming to complete each hole in as few throws as possible by tossing specialized discs toward a target, typically a metal basket with hanging chains. The sport is enjoyed in outdoor settings, offering a blend of strategy, athleticism, and camaraderie for players of all skill levels.
